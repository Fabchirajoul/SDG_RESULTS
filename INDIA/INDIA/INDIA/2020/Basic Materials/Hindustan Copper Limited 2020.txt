contractor through MDO (Mine Developer cum Operator) route for re-opening and expansion of Rakha Copper Mine.

Plant at ICC is in the process of floating the tender for engagement of MDO agency.

drilling has been done during the year 2019-20.

company will take up enhancement of ore production capacity to 20.2 MTPA in 2nd phase after successful completion

of 12.2 million tonne per annum capacity plan.
